InkBox OS installation

There are 3 steps to install InkBox OS on your Kobo eReader: download, flash and setup. To download InkBox, visit the Downloads page.
To flash InkBox to your eReader, take out the internal MicroSD card by taking off the back cover (be careful!). It must have at least 4 gigabytes of storage. If there is less, a new MicroSD card will need to be acquired. Insert the card into your computer.
On Windows, use balenaEtcher to flash the InkBox image file to the MicroSD card.
On Linux and MacOS, balenaEtcher is the easiest option for command-line-reluctant users. Alternatively, you can also open a terminal and check the device node of the eReader's MicroSD card with fdisk -l. Then, write the image to the SD card, like this: 

xzcat inkbox.xz | dd of=/dev/mmcblk0

To setup InkBox, eject the MicroSD card from your computer and insert it in the Kobo's slot. Put the back cover on and power it on. It might take a while to boot, so be patient. Setting up InkBox is easy; just follow the instructions on the screen.
InkBox has been installed! Now you can update InkBox to the latest version.

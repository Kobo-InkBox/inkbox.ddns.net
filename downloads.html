Download InkBox OS

This page contains direct download links for InkBox OS. Huge thanks to @fermino
To check the model number, look at the bottom or back of your eReader.If your model number is not on this page, unfortunately your eReader is not supported by InkBox at this time.
To see if InkBox can be ported to your eReader model, visit the Discord server or the GitHub repository.

  • Kobo Glo (N613): Download InkBox 1.5 (inkbox-1.5-n613.xz)
  • Kobo Mini (N705): Download InkBox 1.5 (inkbox-1.5-n613.xz)
  • Kobo Touch (N905B): Download InkBox 1.7 (inkbox-1.-n905b.xz)
  • Kobo Touch (N905C): Download InkBox 1.5 (inkbox-1.5-905c.xz)
